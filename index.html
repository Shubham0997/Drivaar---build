What is Drivaar?

DSPs have it tough. Managing multiple apps and software to partially automate your business is simply not… modern. Drivaar enables Delivery Service Providers to have an all-in-one solution where you can automate your entire business effectively

Features

Drivaar Offers Diversity and Mobility

Audit & Compliance

Our compliance and auditing dashboard enables you to test everything important. You can check a variety of datasets, including the driver and vehicle data, to make sure everything is in order and there are no issues that require your attention.

Interactive Dashboards

Our dashboards are designed with usability and accessibility in mind. Our variety of dashboards includes compliance dashboard, fleet management dashboard, automation, performance, and much more.

Financial Toolkit

We make sure all your financial needs are covered and automated. With Drivaar, you can cover vehicle rental & insurance against driver earnings, assign bonuses, create invoices, handle the taxes, have access to card payments, email or send notifications to drivers

Accounts Management

The Drivaar accounts management dashboard lets you handle all your payments in an easy way. Your accounts receivable and accounts payable are managed in one place where you have complete control.

Ticketing System

Our Ticketing Support System enables you to create, assign, and handle tickets in a more effective way. The tickets can be sorted department-wise and can be directly sent to the respective manager.

Multitenancy

Drivaar is multitenant software that enables it to be used in a shared environment. All users of your business, whether they be managers or drivers, would be connected through the same server.
